PAN Card Photo Requirements

Guidelines for PAN Card Photo

  • Recent color photograph with plain white or light background
  • Size: 3.5 cm × 2.5 cm (35 mm × 25 mm) or 213 x 213 pixels for online submission
  • Format: JPEG only
  • File size: Maximum 30 KB
  • Resolution: 200–300 DPI
  • Face should be clearly visible with neutral expression and eyes open
  • No hats or accessories (except religious headgear)
